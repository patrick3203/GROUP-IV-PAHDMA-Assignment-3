Project Description

Our group project is a two-way Smart Mirror designed for individuals who want extra assistance in their everyday life at home, whilst maximising the space in their house. Controlled via voice command or manually by mobile/smartwatch, this new technology is easily accessible by its users. The idea is to create a “main” mirror which preferably resides in the most frequented room, while smaller mirrors should be located in different rooms around the house, giving individuals complete control over their mirror no matter where they are and optimising the mirror’s use.

The Smart Mirror includes many available features that we believe will bring convenience to our users. These features include:

  • Lighting control: Using a compatible smart bulb, users are able to control the lighting in any room.
  • Heating & cooling control: With a compatible heating or cooling system, this gives users the option to use the temperature control feature.
  • Security control: Smart locks and security cameras are accessible through the Smart Mirror and mobile device. Users can lock/unlock doors and view their security footage within the mirror. If the mirror believes there is suspicious activity, it will alert the user and the user can choose to call emergency services or ignore the alert.
  • Calendar & reminders: A calendar application allows users to set up a schedule, set reminders and add events, giving them a smarter personalised schedule. Since the mirror is synced with devices, reminders are also sent to their mobiles and smartwatches.
  • News: Local news reports can be viewed upon user command, which is displayed in text form or video.
  • Weather: The Smart Mirror will have an in-built local weather forecast that will also display warnings in case of emergencies such as floods, earthquakes and extreme weather.
  • Health/Fitness and Sleep analysis: Users can track their health, fitness and sleep on their personal device, which the Smart Mirror can access, then giving feedback and recommendations to assist in reaching goals and optimal health/fitness.
  • Music: The user is able to play music via their preferred music app and connect with smart speakers.
  • Smartwatch feature: A special feature included is a smartwatch feature. The smartwatch tracks the user's heart rate and is able to understand their emotions and health. From this, the watch will ask permission to play music on the Smart Mirror based on their mood. Another benefit is health tracking. If the user shows signs of emergency, it will alert emergency contacts on their mobile as well as alert the Smart Mirror, which will display the user’s location for other users at home to see.

To run a home based Smart Mirror to assist users in as many ways as possible, a more advanced software is needed. In regards to hardware, it is vaguely similar to computers. We would need a display screen, RAM, SSD and a motherboard. Furthermore, the use of speakers and microphones is required.
