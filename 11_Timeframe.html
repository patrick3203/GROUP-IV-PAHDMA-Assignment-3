Timeframe

Week Tasks Summary
1
  • Meet with group to discuss the requirements for assignment 3
  • Create a checklist and a planned structure for assignment 3
During week 1, the group will meet up together and look through the requirements for assignment 3. In the meeting, the group will look through assignment 3 specifications and go through each section one by one to gain an understanding of what should be written in each section. Once we have a brief understanding of what should be written in each section, we will create a checklist of what needs to be completed in the week or by the next team meeting.
2
  • Review the feedback that was received in assignment 2 and understand what we did well and what we could improve on in the current assignment
  • Allocating the jobs and roles within the group
  • Discuss what are the goals of our project and work on what could be the unique feature of our product.
 In week 2, the group will reflect on what was done well in assignment 2 and what could have been improved. By reflecting and obtaining the feedback from assignment 2 means that we can turn what we did badly on in the previous assignment and fix it in the current assignment. After when we have looked back on the pros and cons of our previous assignment, we would discuss with the roles that each individuals would like to do or allocate roles that fit to the individual's past experience. By allocating the roles/jobs to each team member of their liking, will mean that they will enjoy doing the task given and will complete it to the best of their ability. Once the roles/jobs are allocated, the group will discuss with one another of what would make the Smart Mirror unique in comparison to other products like Google Home.
3
  • Conclude the writing of pending sections
  • Finalise sections by editing and proofreading
 During week 3, the group members will come together and update on what have been started on or what has been completed that was given in the previous weeks. If any members are unable to complete the given work, any of members in the group that have some extra time on their hands can help assist the individual that needs help to ensure that work is spread out equally and fairly. When majority of the tasks are completed, we will allocate some individuals to edit the sections of work in the assignment, ensuring that it fits the assignment specifications and edit out words or spelling to make it more coherent.
4
  • Review all the documents submitted on the Git repository
  • Deployment of the website with the documents
  • Final check and sumbit assignment
 This is week 4 of the project where all documents for assignment 3 should have been edited, proof-read and improved to then by added in the groups repository in each individual's branch to reflect the work done by each individual. Once all the documents are uploaded into the groups respository, a member will bring all files and documents into the master branch where all the completed work will be brought together. Another individual will take all the documents and files from the master branch and put it together on a AWS website where they will host a HTML website that contains CSS and design the website to look neat and tidy. The files and documents will then be added to the website which will then be double checked and be submitted to the assignment 3 link to the group website.
5
  • Build a prototype and a plan for user testing (Physical or paper)
  • Create a survey to get feedback from the user whether it is online or face-to-face interview (Get their consent to conduct the testing and survey)
 In week 5, we would create a physical or paper protoype to create an understand of how it will look, what are the functions of the mirror and how will it help assist the end user. The very first prototype will be simple to ensure that it is not overcomplicated and easy to understand for everyone. After creating the protoype, we would need to receive feedback from random indivduals, which will be done through beta testing, to understand their perspective of the Smart Mirror and use the feedback to improve the prototype. We would ensure to ask for their consent if they would like to take part of the face-to-face interview or online survey to ensure that their private information is secured and obtain the necessary feedback to improve the product.
6
  • Analysing the initial feedback received from user understanding what features need to be worked on
  • Planning on how to approach the new changes
  • Improve the prototype with the feedback received
 In week 6, the plan is to use the feedback from the previous week and come back with the group to discuss what they have recieved as feedback, what could be improved and what was good, and discuss what could be done to avoid or fix the product that users did not particularly like. By ulitising the feedback, we can now create a brief understanding of what the user wants in the end products and what they would like to be in it. We will discuss as a group on how to approach and implement these new changes to the Smart Mirror. Adapting from the first prototype and improving it based on the feedback received, we will have a new and improved prototype to see if any users like or dislike.
7
  • Further testing with the new and improved changes
  • Monitor feedback and check on progress
 Once we have implemented the feedback received from those participants that were willing to take part of the prototype testing, we would go back and ask randoms if they would like to participate in the testing to further improve the prototype. We would then need to check on the feedback if they fit the criteria of the results to be used and seperate the criticism received. We would then need to update on what the users like and what they dislike in order to improvoe our prototyping.
8
  • Understand the progress that has been made in the project
  • Check with each group member if they are happy with the roles that they are given out and if not, try to switch some roles around to allow all members in the group to do what they would like to do
 During week 8, the group will come back together and discuss how the project is progressing from the previous 3 weeks. This is so that if the group is behind or ahead of the project timeline, they are able to continue moving forward or catch up to ensure that they can try complete the main product at the end of week 14. The roles of the prototype of the Smart Mirror will be allocated to ensure that every individual of the group are happy and willing to do their role. If not, then the roles will be shifted around to ensure that everyone gets a role they want as they will then complete the task/job to the best of their ability.
9
  • Further research on relevant tools, techniques and technologies to improve and refine the project
 During this week, we will meet up again as group and use the feedback from the second prototype and research on the tools required, techniques and technologies that users want and improved to refine the project. Once individuals have researched on the information required, we will come back as a group and explain to one another who, what, when, where and how it will be impleted in the Smart Mirror.
10
  • Create an understanding of the skills that each member has so that the roles and tasks can be given out based on the members experience in the area
  • Discuss with the group on how to implement the feedback received and more information based in the previous week
 See if any members would like to work on tasks with another individual based on their prior experiences in the related area. This can help ease the workload and pressure of another individual of research or learning more things during this time period which may affect on how well they do in their personal section of the work given. Use the feedback recieved from the previous weeks and find more information that could be used to refine the project and make it unique compared to other companies that produces similiar products to make it stand out. Understand what users want as the product is design for them, not for yourself and use the information receivedd to see how to implement what users requested or improved in to the Smart Mirror project.
11
  • Create a final prototype to receive final feedback to then be used to create the end product, the Smart Mirror
  • Discuss the final feedback received from the final prototype
  • When completed the discussion, hand our roles or who wants to do what with the group members
 In week 11, the group will create one final prototype to receive as much feedback based on the 2nd prototype created. We will then go back to user testing or beta testing and see if users will like the final prototype that uses all feedback received from prototype 1 and 2. We will then receive the final feedback which will then be discussed as a group and see how we can include what the users really want to see within our product. We will then hand out other roles that are created to each individual group member to see what fits to their previous experience or what not and dedicate what roles each individuals would like to do.
12
  • Collect findings from feedback and apply to end product
  • Update final report and other documentation
  • Update the progress made with the Smart Mirror with the group
 We will then collect our findings from the final prototype and apply it to the end product, the Smart Mirror. We will need to ensure that the Smart Mirror meets the users requirements and expectations by implementing the features they would like to be added into the project. We will then update our progress with the progress and update the documentation as a group and discuss how we will build the Smart Mirror and complete what will be needed in the Raspberry Pi.
13
  • Group meeting, discuss what final things need to be done
  • Last product test, check if it meets up to all standards and satisfies users needs from feedback
 The group will finalise what needs to be completed, through a checklist, and allocate the roles to each individual group member they would like to do. We will then need to ensure that our product will fit or meet the users standards and satifies the users needs from the feedback from the prototypes created. The group will then update with the group in the following week and ensure that all the checklist things are completed.
14
  • Finalise and review final report and other documentations
  • Finalise the end product and produce the product
 This is the final week of the Smart Mirror project where as a group, we need to finalise, update and review on our reports and final documentations. This would then be proof read, edited and checked by more than one individual to ensure that the documentation is to the best of the ability. We will then go and finialise our final product design and then do ahead and start building the project to be completed within the week.
